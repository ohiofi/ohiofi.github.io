These cubbies keep the students' recorders organized. Each child has a number that corresponds to a cubbyhole. With three classes playing recorders at the same time, no more than three recorders and books will be in one hole. On top of this unit, I keep student papers, frequently used DVDs and resources, and items needed for games and activities such as flashcards.
