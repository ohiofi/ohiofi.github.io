This cabinet holds all of our small instruments. The drawers are labeled with the type of instrument that goes inside (e.g., tappers - wood, tappers - metal, shakers, scrapers, etc.). This makes getting things out and putting them away a snap. You can just see behind the left door more Boomwhackers in the corner. I'm trying to figure out a spot to store these using an idea I have seen on Pinterest (see the links at the bottom of this blog post).
