My desk sits between the SMART Board and the stereo cabinet. My computer is hooked into the stereo so when we watch videos or I play music from the computer, it plays through my good, wall mounted stereo speakers. The bulletin board above my desk features my favorite musical and educational lols, a couple of student reminders (no whining!), and curriculum materials. Above that on the wall is my class schedule. I've ditched the desk chair for an exercise ball this year. The kids always want to sit on it, too.
