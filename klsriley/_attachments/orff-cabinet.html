Most of my pitched Orff instruments fit nicely inside this cabinet. I need to re-label a couple of the shelves for some newly purchased instruments. Usually, I have the instruments out and ready before class if we are using them, and I stand at the cabinet and put them back on the shelves as the students carry them over after use.
