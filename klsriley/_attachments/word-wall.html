For a few years now, I have had a music word wall in my classroom. This is actually butcher paper covering a large magnetic staff whiteboard. I got the great little pushpin magnets at Staples. I love that I can change and rearrange the words without poking holes in them or in the paper, and I can leave this up year after year! I write the words with colored marker on sentence strips and cut them to size. The words are added to the wall as we learn them in class.
