Here is a close up of part of my Composer of the Month timeline that hangs above the bulletin boards. The string is just black acrylic yarn. It is attached to the panel with command hooks and teeny little zip ties. It's held on for three years! I simply clip the laminated photos of our composers to it with clothespins. Each era is color coded for easy identification.
