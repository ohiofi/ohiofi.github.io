  1. Open the spreadsheet in Google Docs
  2. Click File, then click Publish To The Web
  3. Click the Start Publishing button
  4. Underneath "Get a link to the published data" click on Webpage and change it to HTML To Embed In A Page
  5. Copy and paste the iframe code into your blog
