The concept of a feedback organ is nothing new, but I really like the way that this performer uses hand motions to bend the pitch.

The Feedback Organ from Neptune on Vimeo.

This video is a brief introduction to an instrument called "The Feedback Organ." Built by J. S. Sanford, the instrument can be heard on Neptune's recent record, "msg rcvd," available from the record label, Northern Spy.
