Circuit Bent NES ( Famicom ) with LTC1799 Pitch Mod

If you ever wondered what happens after you install the GetLoFi LTC1799 Precision Oscillator in the original 8 bit Nintendo then here is your answer. The sounds can be slowed down and sped up nicely at a twist of a knob, and when paired up with some glitches it really makes for a crazy noise making instrument. Thanks to Kaseo for sending this in!
